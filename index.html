Our Story

Founded in 2015 by three close friends in Bandung, West Java, Fruclate was born from a shared passion for crafting unique chocolate experiences. Our vision was simple yet ambitious — to create premium-quality chocolate infused with the natural essence of fruits, offering a distinctive taste that delights the senses.

Over the years, Fruclate has grown from a small home-based venture into a trusted brand loved by chocolate enthusiasts across the country. Each product is carefully crafted using only the finest cocoa beans and handpicked fruits, ensuring a harmonious blend of rich chocolate flavor and refreshing fruity notes.

At Fruclate, we believe that chocolate is more than just a treat — it’s a celebration of moments, emotions, and creativity. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and authenticity continues to drive us forward as we share our creations with the world.
